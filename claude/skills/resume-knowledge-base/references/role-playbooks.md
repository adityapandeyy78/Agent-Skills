# Role Playbooks — Data Analyst · Founder's Office · Product · Consulting

A resume is built **for one role family**. Each playbook gives: the angle, what to
lead with, the keyword bank to mirror (only claim what the profile supports), what
to emphasize, and what to cut. When a JD is provided, the JD's own words override
these defaults — mirror its exact tool names and phrasing.

Recruiters skim ~6 seconds. They want to list your top 3 wins fast. Quantify
60–80% of bullets, show ownership, keep it one page.

---

## 1. Data Analyst
**Angle:** "I turn messy data into decisions." Technical depth + business impact.

**Lead with:** Data Analyst Intern (Analytics Resources) → Projects (F1, Employee
Attrition, Data Warehouse) → Skills. Education near top (new grad).

**Keyword bank (use what's true):** SQL (PostgreSQL, MySQL, BigQuery SQL),
Python (Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn), Excel (Pivot Tables, Power Query,
VLOOKUP/INDEX-MATCH), Tableau, Power BI, Looker, ETL (Extract, Transform, Load),
data cleaning, EDA, statistical analysis, A/B testing, KPI reporting, dashboards,
data modeling, star schema, data warehouse, cohort/segmentation analysis,
forecasting, GA4. Spell acronyms once.

**Emphasize:** scale (10K+, 25K+ records), accuracy %, the specific stack per
bullet, business outcome (profit/pricing/retention). Mention named tools, not
"data visualization software."

**Cut / shrink:** Founder's Office strategy fluff, content-writing roles, PR.
Case studies optional (keep only data-heavy ones, or drop). Growth role → keep
only its analytics bullets.

---

## 2. Founder's Office / Chief-of-Staff
**Angle:** "Trusted generalist who turns founder priorities into shipped
outcomes." Range + ownership + business sense.

**Lead with:** Gentle Money (Founder's Office) → Omelo (Growth & Strategy) →
Data Analyst → Projects/Case studies. Show breadth across product, ops, GTM,
finance.

**Keyword bank:** strategy, business planning, monetization, GTM, market
research, competitor benchmarking, pricing analysis, unit economics, financial
modeling, revenue forecasting, fundraising/pitch decks, business intelligence,
stakeholder management, cross-functional, OKRs, operations, 0→1.

**Emphasize:** working **directly with the founder**, breadth of surface area,
business/revenue impact, judgment, and that she ships (MRDs, models, decks, BI
reports). Founder's-office readers look for unit-economics detail to judge
scaling sense — surface it where she has it (ask if unsure).

**Cut / shrink:** deep technical library lists, pure content/PR-only roles
(compress to one line if kept).

---

## 3. Product Management (PM / APM)
**Angle:** "Product thinker who pairs user empathy with data." Product lifecycle
coverage + outcomes.

**Lead with:** Gentle Money (product strategy) + Omelo (growth/PM-adjacent) →
Projects + Case studies (JioMart redesign, AI Trading) → Skills (Product Sense
first).

**Keyword bank:** product strategy, roadmap, PRD/MRD, user research, user
journeys, wireframing, MVP, feature prioritization, A/B testing, retention,
activation, funnel, North Star metric, OKRs, agile/scrum, sprint planning,
stakeholder management, go-to-market, competitive analysis.

**Emphasize:** outcomes (retention +40%, adoption +30%), user-feedback → feature
translation, product sense via case studies, cross-functional leadership.

**Cut / shrink:** heavy DB/library lists (keep a short analytics line — data-savvy
PM is a plus). PR-only roles compress.

---

## 4. Consulting (strategy / management)
**Angle:** "Structured problem-solver with quantified impact." Firms buy smart
brains that drive impact — show you're smart, structured, and a leader.

**Lead with:** Education (CGPA + any test scores) high → Gentle Money (strategy)
→ Omelo → Projects/Case studies → Leadership. A recruiter should name your top 3
achievements in 15 seconds.

**Keyword bank:** market analysis, competitive benchmarking, business strategy,
financial modeling, data-driven recommendations, problem-solving, hypothesis-
driven, stakeholder management, client/operations impact, process optimization,
go-to-market.

**Emphasize:** quantified impact on **60–80%** of bullets, 2–3 clear
leadership/ownership examples, brand names where impressive, structured
problem-solving narrative. Crisp, no jargon padding.

**Cut / shrink:** long tool/library lists (consulting cares less about tooling),
content/social-media roles.

---

## Section-order cheatsheet (new grad)
- **Data Analyst:** Header → Education → Experience → Projects → Skills → Certs
- **Founder's Office:** Header → Experience → Projects/Case Studies → Skills →
  Education → Leadership
- **Product:** Header → Experience → Projects → Case Studies → Skills → Education
- **Consulting:** Header → Education → Experience → Projects → Leadership → Skills

For experienced candidates, Experience always precedes Education.
